Essay

My name is Sofiya, I am 19 years old. Since childhood, I have loved art and intellectual games. I have been playing chess since I was three years old, and this game has become an integral part of my life. In high school, I organized and judged chess tournaments at the regional and national levels, striving for the title of orbiter. I was only two tournaments short of achieving this goal, but the experience I gained was invaluable. I really like to draw and prefer abstractionism and surrealism. These styles allow me to express inner emotions and ideas through unusual shapes and colors. Two of my paintings were sent to an exhibition in Belarus, which was an important achievement for me and motivated me to develop further in this direction. Another of my passions is music. During my school years, I was a rhythm guitarist in a musical group, where we performed our favorite compositions and created our own. Besides music and drawing, I find inspiration in reading books, which are portals to other worlds for me.
